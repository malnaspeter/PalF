Privacy Policy for PálferiApp
Introduction
Welcome to PálferiApp! We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their personal data. This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, storage, and protection of your data while using our app.

PálferiApp does not collect any personal data. The app only requires internet access to download audio content and to track user interactions such as listened audio content and favorites. These data are processed anonymously and do not directly identify the user.

The data collected are used for the following purposes:

To download audio content and enhance user experience.
To track user preferences to offer personalized content.
To analyze and improve the app’s performance and stability.
Data Storage
The data collected by the app are securely stored on the device. We take all necessary measures to protect the data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Privacy Rights
